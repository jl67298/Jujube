Entrepreneur and innovator, Jujube is currently redesigning a more sustainable future for travelers by applying artificial intelligence to the intersection of travel, apparel and carbon emissions reduction.


Jujube has experience establishing and managing an eponymous sustainable apparel label based on the modular apparel design system she invented. Modular apparel design reduces raw material consumption and storage space exponentially by turning whole garments into separate modules within a collection that generate an exponential number of silhouettes. All her designs were locally manufactured using EU eco-certified textiles. Within a year of launching the label's e-commerce site, Jujube successfully debuted her capsule collections on the runway and launched the label's first brick-and-mortar retail operations.
